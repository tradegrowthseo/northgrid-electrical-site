Commercial Electrical Installation

What we install — and how we work

NorthGrid Electrical Ltd handles the full scope of commercial electrical installation — from a single new circuit or distribution board upgrade through to complete rewires, large-scale commercial fit-outs and ongoing FM support contracts. Every job is carried out by Matt Foster or an engineer under his direct supervision, never subcontracted.

Our approach is built around compliance first. Every installation is designed and executed to BS 7671 / 18th Edition standards, with full certification on completion — an Electrical Installation Certificate (EIC) for new work or a Minor Works Certificate where applicable. We work with facilities managers, housing associations, retail operators and commercial property owners who need documentation that passes scrutiny first time.

We understand that downtime costs money. Whether you're a facilities manager juggling a busy estate or a retail operator who cannot close during trading hours, we plan our works around your schedule — including out-of-hours, evenings and weekends where required. Matt has specialist experience in live-environment electrical installation, with risk assessments and RAMS supplied as standard for complex or occupied sites.

Same-day quotes are available for most straightforward works. For larger projects, we carry out a site survey and provide a fully itemised fixed-price quote in writing — no day-rate billing, no variation surprises.

Why commercial clients choose NorthGrid

  • 15+ years' experience in commercial and industrial electrical installation across the North West and nationwide.
  • BS 7671 / 18th Edition compliant on every installation — EIC or Minor Works Certificate issued on completion.
  • In-house labour only. Matt's engineers attend every job — no unknown subcontractors on your site.
  • Live-environment specialists. Aviation-cleared, retail-cleared and experienced in occupied premises — full RAMS supplied.
  • Single point of contact from initial enquiry through to final certification and handover.

Installation Services

New Circuits & Sub-Circuits

Additional power and lighting circuits for new equipment, extensions or layout changes. Full design, installation and EIC certification.

Distribution Board Upgrades

Consumer unit and distribution board replacements including SPD (surge protection) and RCBO protective devices, to current 18th Edition requirements.

Three-Phase Installations

Three-phase supply installations and upgrades for industrial plant, heavy equipment and high-demand commercial premises.

Commercial Fit-Outs & Refurbishments

Full electrical installation for new commercial fit-outs — offices, retail units, restaurants and co-working spaces — from first fix to completion.

Lighting & Emergency Lighting

Internal, external and emergency lighting installations — LED conversions, maintained emergency fittings and full BS EN 1838 compliant emergency lighting schemes.

Fire Alarm Installations (Aico)

Fire alarm and smoke detection installations using Aico systems. Standalone and interlinked detectors for commercial and residential settings. Full documentation supplied.

EV Charger Cabling & Installations

Commercial-scale EV charging infrastructure — dedicated supply circuits, EVCP cabling, chargepoint installations and load management for car parks and fleet facilities.

Earthing & Bonding Upgrades

Main and supplementary equipotential bonding upgrades, earth electrode installation and earthing system remediation to meet BS 7671 requirements.

Live-Environment Works

Electrical installation in occupied premises — it's what we're known for

Most electricians will tell you a site needs to be vacated before electrical work can begin. NorthGrid takes a different approach. Matt Foster has spent 15+ years developing the planning, isolation and sequencing skills needed to carry out electrical installation work in occupied, trading and safety-critical environments — without shutting the site down.

We've worked in live retail units, operational offices, aviation facilities and high-security sites where downtime is not an option. Every live-environment project is preceded by a full risk assessment and an Isolation Plan, and we supply a complete RAMS package as standard.

Whether you need a distribution board upgrade completed overnight, new circuits installed across a retail fit-out that's trading the next morning, or phased works on an occupied industrial unit — we structure the job around your operational requirements.

How NorthGrid minimises your downtime

  • Phased works planned around your operating hours
  • Out-of-hours and weekend working available
  • Isolation planning and sequenced switching
  • Aviation-cleared and retail site experience
  • High-security site vetting and DBS where required
  • Full RAMS documentation supplied as standard
